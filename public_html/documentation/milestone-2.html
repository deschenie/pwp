Content Strategy

This site will be one page, and will consist of the following sections.

Welcome

A brief one-two line introduction that clearly states my name and current role. This message will be overlaid on a large background image of the four corners.

About Me

This section will detail my professional and art/design background, along with my skill set. I will feature my web development specific skills here. My tone will be casual yet informative.

Art/Design Portfolio

I will have ten-fifteen photos from examples of photography, graphic design, drawing, and painting to showcase my experience in Art/Design. I will make note of commission, assignment, and personal work.

Web Development

I will have a text area explaining my experience with the Deep Dive Bootcamp. Then I will have two examples of work I completed in the bootcamp tha will include my contributions to each project. I will have responsive links to the Capstone example, and three photos of the app.

Contact Me

This section will contain a contact form (to ask a question or request a resume) and links to my professional social media profiles (LinkedIn, GitHub).
